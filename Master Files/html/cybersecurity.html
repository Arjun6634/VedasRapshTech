Cyber-security Course

Course Overview

Our Cybersecurity course provides essential training to understand, identify, and mitigate security threats. You will gain the skills needed to secure networks, protect data, and respond to cyber attacks using modern tools and techniques.

What You Will Learn:

  • Fundamentals of network and information security
  • Cyber attack detection and prevention techniques
  • Ethical hacking and penetration testing
  • Data protection and encryption
  • Incident response and risk management

Course Duration:

10 weeks, with flexible learning options.

Prerequisites:

No prior experience is required, but a basic understanding of networking concepts is beneficial.
